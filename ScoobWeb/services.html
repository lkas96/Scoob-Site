What services does Scoob provide?











Schools



- School Admins are able to access the Scoob Admin Portal in the web

- Manage information of teachers, classes & students

- Bulk import of information available too








Transport Companies



- Transport Admins are able to access the Scoob Admin Portal in the web

- Manage information of buses & drivers

- Assign drivers to buses, assign routes of bus trips etc.

- Bulk import of information available too








Teachers



- Scan QR code for pickup verification in the Mobile App

- View pickup information








Parents



- Subscribe to Bus Pickup & change pickup modes in the Mobile App

- Alert school upon arrival, indicate child(s) to pickup

- Authorisation of 3rd-Party Personnel for pickup available too
















Drivers



- View student passenger list in the Mobile App

- Start & End trips to update statuses
